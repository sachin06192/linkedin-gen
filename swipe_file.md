# Swipe File — Viral LinkedIn Post Analysis

Collect 20-30 high-engagement LinkedIn posts that feel authentic (not corporate).
Use Cowork to browse LinkedIn and capture these. Focus on posts from your niche:
AI, trading, startups, dev tools, building in public.

---

## How to Capture Posts

For each post, fill in this template:

```
## Post N
- **Author:** [name, role/title]
- **Followers:** ~[estimate]
- **Likes/Comments:** ~X / ~Y
- **Image type:** tweet screenshot | chart/data | carousel | code snippet | product screenshot | meme | none
- **Hook style:** question | bold claim | number/stat | personal story | "I did X" | controversy
- **Length:** short (<100 words) | medium (100-200) | long (200-400)
- **Why it works:** [1-2 sentences on what makes this post feel real/engaging]

**Text:**
[paste the full post text here]

**Image description:** [describe the image/screenshot if any]
```

---

## What to Look For

**Good signals (save these):**
- Posts with 500+ likes OR 50+ comments
- Tweet screenshots that sparked discussion
- "Free resources" lists with real links and specific details
- Personal failure/learning stories that feel raw
- Build-in-public updates with real numbers
- Posts where the author clearly used/tested what they're writing about
- Unexpected formatting (short punchy lines, one-word paragraphs, etc.)

**Skip these:**
- Corporate announcements ("Excited to share...")
- Generic motivational quotes
- Engagement bait ("Comment YES if you agree")
- Carousel slideshows about "10 AI tools you need"
